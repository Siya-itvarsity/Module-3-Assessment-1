Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Prep Time: 10 mins

Yield : 4

Ingredients

1/4 cup butter, softened
1tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese
8 Italian bread slices
4 (3/4.oz.) Provolone cheese slices
4 (3/4.oz) Mozzarela chees slices

Steps

  1. 1. Stir together butter and Parmesan cheese in a small bowl.
  2. 2. Spread 1 1/2 tsp. butter mixture on 1 side of each bread slice. Place 4
  3. 3. Cook sandwich, in batches, on a hot grilled or in a non-stick skillet oven medium heat
